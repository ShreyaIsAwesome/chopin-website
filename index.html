Frédéric Chopin

Frédéric François Chopin was a Polish composer and virtuoso pianistof the Romantic period, who wrote primarily for solo piano. He has maintained worldwide renown as a leading musician of his era, one whose "poetic genius was based on a professional technique that was without equal in his generation".
